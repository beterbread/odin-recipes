Spaghetti

A simple recipe for spaghetti lovers.

This recipe is fast and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Onion and Tomato
  • Olive oil
  • Your preferred seasoning

Steps

  1. Put your noodles in boiling water
  2. Cook ground beef and onion and tomato with olive oil in a seperate pan and season it
  3. Once done cooking, add the sauce to the pan
  4. Simmer the sauce and season to taste
  5. Once noodles are done cooking, add your noodles and sauce in a bowl and serve
