PB&J

A simple recipe for pb&j lovers.

This recipe is fast and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Grape jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on a slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on another slice of bread
  3. Place the slices of bread together so that the peanut butter and jelly are touching
  4. Place on a dish and serve
